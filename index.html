工作方式How It Works

扩展在后台监听网络请求，嗅探到 .m3u8 地址后，弹出下载按钮。点击即可唤起 M3U8Go 客户端并自动填入下载信息（地址、标题、来源页）。支持在扩展设置中选择启用的网站及开启调试浮层。 The extension listens to network requests in the background. When an .m3u8 URL is detected, a download button appears. One click launches M3U8Go and pre-fills the URL, title, and referrer. Configure supported sites and debug overlay in extension options.
